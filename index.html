Blue Whales Japan

One Team One Dream

Blue Whales Japan has a big dream which is creating smiles from people around the world who use the excellent Japanese products we have introduced. In recent years, countries around the world have made great economic progress due to the development of IT technology and domestic infrastructure. Along with that, their standard of living has improved, and their credibility for Japanese products have grown even closer. We believe that the future will be a means of creating new added value for companies by taking a step overseas and appealing not only domestically. Our strength is that we can quickly grasp local needs and sell in cooperation with local companies. Under such circumstances, we will contribute to a Japanese company that wants to steadily expand its business while searching for local information together with us.

OUR SERVICES

Our Services

Blue Whales Japan K.K. was established with the hope of becoming a bridge between Japan and other countries in the world, which are expected to grow even more in the future. Highly safe foods and cosmetics are the first to be used, private cars and industrial heavy machinery that are still valuable, and the most advanced medical equipment and supplies that are essential for people to live richly. In order to quickly detect the needs of these countries, we work with local partner companies and local marketing staff to provide attractive products such as Japanese quality, safety and design that overseas customers can use with peace of mind.
